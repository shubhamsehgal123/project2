How We Collect Information?
Before posting a biography of any person/celebrities. We do research and collect all information from different sources and publish it here. We use leading news websites, Wikipedia, celebrity’s personal website/blog, Social media accounts. We also watch their interviews on youtube and news sites to get more information then we share all information here on celebinfo.wiki.
