Sushant Singh Rajput Wiki, Height, Age, Death, Girlfriend, Wife, Family, Biography & More

Sushant Singh Rajput was born on 21 January 1986 in Maldiha. Maldhia is a village of Purnea district in Bihar, India. Sushant Sing Rajput is a famous Bollywood Actor, dancer. And also a television personality. He started his journey with TV serial “Pavitra Rishta”. His first film is “Kai Po Che!” which is released in 2013.

He started his schooling in Bihar. Later he moved to Delhi. He ranked 7th in DCE entrance exam. Then he took admission in Delhi College of Engineering for being a mechanical engineer. He was a topper in his class. In other words he was star student. However, the above information clearly shows that Sushant was a very bright student in his education time period. He also won Physics National Olympiad and cleared many engineering entrance exams.

Sushant drops out just after 3rd year to pursue an acting career. When Sushant was in college, he was very attracted to dance. Because of this, he joined Shiamak Davar’s dance group. As a result, he went to Australia to attend opening ceremony commonwealth games in the year 2006, and also he performed in the cultural program.
